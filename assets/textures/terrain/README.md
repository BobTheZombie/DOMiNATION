# Terrain Textures

## `grassland_tile_1024.png`
- Resolution: 1024x1024.
- Style: lush green grass with subtle color variation and a patchy natural look.
- Usage: seamless, tileable top-down terrain texture for RTS maps.
- Notes: border pixels are matched on opposite sides to avoid visible seams when tiled.

## `river_water_tile_1024.png`
- Resolution: 1024x1024.
- Style: flowing blue river water with subtle wave motion and lighter shallow-edge highlights.
- Usage: seamless, tileable top-down RTS water texture with strong visual contrast from land tiles.
- Notes: opposite border pixels are matched exactly to ensure seam-free tiling.
